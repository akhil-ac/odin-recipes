Quick and Easy Pasta Salad Recipe

Description

This pasta salad with pepperoni, tomatoes, and provolone cheese is the best I've ever eaten, and people request it frequently. It's a very easy, light-tasting side dish for a picnic or dinner.

In our family this is the go to quick snack that we almost make every other day. The kids are happy to eat their veggies with the pasta and light wieght homemade sauce

Ingredients

  • 1 (16 ounce) package rotini pasta
  • 1/2 pound pepperoni, sliced
  • 1/2 pound provolone cheese, cubed
  • 1/2 cup Italian-style salad dressing
  • 1/2 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 1/4 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1/4 teaspoon dried basil
  • 1/4 teaspoon crushed red pepper flakes (optional)

Instructions

  1. Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Add rotini pasta and cook for 8 to 10 minutes or until al dente; drain.
  2. In a large bowl, combine the cooked pasta, pepperoni, provolone cheese, Italian-style salad dressing, cherry tomatoes, Parmesan cheese, black pepper, garlic powder, oregano, basil, and red pepper flakes (if using).
  3. Toss gently to combine all ingredients. Cover and refrigerate for at least 30 minutes before serving to allow flavors to meld.
  4. Serve chilled or at room temperature.

Notes

This pasta salad can be customized with your favorite vegetables or proteins. Feel free to add bell peppers, olives, or grilled chicken for extra flavor and nutrition.

Enjoy your delicious pasta salad!
